Who we are

Harper & The Hare is a quiet boutique studio in Murwillumbah, founded by Harper Wren after a decade behind chairs in Melbourne and London. Three senior stylists, one room, by appointment only.

Why we exist

We wanted a room where the colour, the cut, and the conversation could all take as long as they needed. Nothing rushed, nothing performed.

Work with us.

A long-form approach to every guest. Less rushed, more considered. Every appointment is built around the way your hair lives, not just the way it looks the day you leave.

01 · Consult

An honest conversation.

Bring references, expect questions, give us time. Nothing is rushed before we agree on a direction.

02 · Slow colour

Painted, not rushed.

Hand-painted lightener, foils, and gloss work, placed with a soft eye and a slow hand.

03 · Cut

A lived-in shape.

Cut dry, refined wet, finished air-dried. The shape should still hold its line three weeks on.

04 · Aftercare

What you take home.

A short, considered routine and the products we actually use behind the chair. Nothing else.
